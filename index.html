About

The Organization

Challenging Heights is an organization promoting children’s rights to education and freedom from forced labor in Ghana. For more information, visit challengingheights.org.




About

The Race

The Rescue Race is a 5k run/walk to support children who are victims or at risk of child slavery and forced child labor in Ghana. By registering for the Race and/or helping to fundraise, you and your friends can help rescue, rehabilitate, educate, and empower these vulnerable children and their communities.

FAQs

Have Questions?

If you can’t find the answer to one of your questions, please email contact@childrensrescuerace.com.

Where do I park?

Parking is available in lots H & G across from the Kirkhof Center: Click for Map

How can I become more involved with Challenging Heights in the area?

Contact GR Regional Board President, Stephany Zahl at stephany@childrensrescuerace.com

How can I volunteer?

Click HERE to email about a volunteer position! We need course directors, water station volunteers, and day-of registration and packet pick-up coordinators.

Are there age groups/age requirements for the race?

Nope, all ages are encouraged and welcome!

What are the registration deadlines?

• Register by race day for general fee!
• Register by October 9th to receive a T-shirt!

What is included in my registration?

• T-Shirt (Register by October 10th)
• Race Bib and Pins
• Race Packet

How do I know if my registration is submitted correctly?

You will receive a confirmation email after your registration is submitted!

What will be provided at the aid stations?

• Water will be provided during the race!
• Water and snacks will be provided at the finish line!
